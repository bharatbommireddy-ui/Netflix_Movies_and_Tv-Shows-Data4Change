Netflix Movies and Tv-Shows Data Analysis

An analysis of Netflix movies and tv-shows and their genres.

View Graphs →

Problem Statement

Due to the vast amount of content in Netflix, many viewer find it difficult to find somthing to watch of their own interest in their prefered genre. But is there a way to solve this? This project analasizes the top prefered genres acording to viewer of all over the world.

Research Questions

Question 1

What are the top ten movie genres watched and how many are there are in each genre

Question 2

What are the top ten tv-shows genres watched and how many are there are in each genre.

Question 3

How much does the content in each genre take up out of the total content in the Netflix streaming service.

Conclusion

In conclusion, while Netflix’s expansive library often leads to choice paralysis, analyzing global genre trends offers a clear path toward more efficient and personalized viewing.
